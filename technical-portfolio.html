Technical Portfolio

In Summer 2020 I had a 2 week internship with Novitech Digital, an app design company in Pretoria, South Africa.

They had created a app for one of the major Health Insurance Providers in South Africa.

The app connected Doctors to their patients securely and quickly, which was phenomenal in the midst of rampant Covid.

It worked really well and doctors were able to remotely video call and prescribe medicine to their patients.

However, they had a problem...

Their corporate managers wanted to be able to send notifications. Corporate managers...

And the way to send notifications was through an API URL path...

That is where I came in!!!

I built a front-end for their notification API URL Path with fields that they could edit, animations and previews for each type of notication.

It was responsive and easy to use. And it's still in use!

In the years since I first created it, it has been used to send messages to tens of thousands of their users!
