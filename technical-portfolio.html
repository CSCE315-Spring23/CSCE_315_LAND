Novitech Digital Internship

In Summer 2020 I had a 2 week internship with Novitech Digital, an app design company in Pretoria, South Africa.

    Novitech Digital's Website

They had created a app for Bonitas, one of the major Health Insurance Providers in South Africa.

    Bonitas App

The app connected Doctors to their patients securely and quickly, which was phenomenal in the midst of rampant Covid.

It worked really well and doctors were able to remotely video call and prescribe medicine to their patients.

However, they had a problem...

Their corporate managers wanted to be able to send notifications. Corporate managers...

And the way to send notifications was through an API URL path...

That is where I came in!!!

I built a front-end for their notification API URL Path with fields that they could edit, animations and previews for each type of notication.

It was responsive and easy to use. And it's still in use!

In the years since I first created it, it has been used to send messages to tens of thousands of their users!

Heart Shaped Parenting Course App

In 2020 I spent 3 months working for my parents on their Course App Web.

They have a ministry named Heart-Shaped Parenting where they teach people about Christian Parenting

They wrote a book and from that book they had created several video courses that people could watch.

However, they did not like any of the course platforms that were currently in existence.

So, they hired me to design and build an online course app for them.

This platform had to allow users to purchase courses that would be added to their account.

Then they could stream these courses from our web app and the app would keep track of their progress through the course.

I built both the front and back end for this application. Using Flutter to create the front-end and firebase to host the account details, web app and videos on the back-end.

After 3 months it was fully functional and to this day it has been used for individuals and churches to stream their content.
